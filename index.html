Ball Game

Instructions!

When you hit the start button, a random number of balls will appear with an assigned value.

White = 1, Yellow = 2, Orange = 3, Red = 4, and Green = 5.

Your task is to add up all of the values and submit your answer before the balls hit the edge.

You can do this by typing you answer into the input field and wither hitting the enter key or the "Answer!" button.

If you fail, the game will end and you must refresh the page to restart. If you succeed, your points will go up.

Your score will show up here!
