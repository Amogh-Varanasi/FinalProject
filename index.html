Instructions!

When you hit the start button, a random number of balls will appear with an assigned value.

White = 1, Yellow = 2, Orange = 3, Red = 4, and Green = 5.

Your task is to add up all of the values and enter your answer before the balls hit the edge.

If you fail, the game will end and you must refresh the oage to restart. If you succeed, your points will go up.

Your score will show up here!
